CrackCheck

An AI based Crack Detection Solution

It starts with a single CRACK.

Crack, small enough to be ignored, big enough to cause a catastrophe. CrackCheck, an AI-powered solution, helps you to detect even the smallest of cracks. CrackCheck can detect cracks from live footage and in places where human reach is near impossible. Thus, helps us in saving countless lives.

Motivation

The process of inspection of bridges and other public infrastructure is a tedious, laborious and time consuming. It is often seen that the repair of the facilities is not at par with the stipulated values. And further delay in detection and repair of the damaged portions puts the user of facilities in grave danger. Sometimes this delay leads to unfortunate incidents leading to loss of life and property. We aim to build a product which can help minimise the time and effort put into damage detection to a minimal amount.

How it Works?

The footages from Drone, Android Phone or any other Camera is send to CrackCheck which analyzes these footages and detect the crack with its precise location. CrackCheck uses highly sophisticated algorithms to detect the crack in runtime. This detection can be done either in Runtime,i.e, from live footages or stored videos.

Future Plans

Currently our solution targets drone and other mobile mediums to scan the bridge and detect the cracks. In future iterations we plan to expand the horizon of CrackCheck to detect cracks in other marvellous structures including building, parking lots, and other real estate structures. Our future projects target to detect anomalies in Steel structures too. Our solutions will eventually include vibration-based sensing which would allow us to get more insight about the problems in the structures.Another field we hope to employ with our product is that of hydraulic structures such as dams, barrages, weirs and spillways. We hope that our product will revolutionize the field of Structural health monitoring and help us save countless lives.
